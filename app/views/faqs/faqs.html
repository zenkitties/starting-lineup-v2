What are your shipping policies?

PLEASE NOTE: The Starting Lineup is a DROP SHIPPER, we do NOT carry inventory. How does this work? When we get an order, we email the supplier, and they typically ship within 2-3 business days. PLEASE NOTE: there are some vendors who build their product upon receiving an order from us, and may take 5-7 business days to ship, so please be patient. They do this to keep your cost down.

As soon as our email goes out, then you'll get an "Awaiting Shipment" email from us. We then pass along the tracking information from the supplier as soon as we get it, to you, via email.

Most domestic orders are shipped using UPS Ground. We aim to ship your order on the day that it is received (from 2 business days, up to 7) and processed with the following exceptions: orders received during weekends, holidays, or from December through February may be slightly delayed.

There are times of the year such as December through February our suppliers "guess" at how many products to keep in inventory, but unfortunately cannot see into the future and orders may be delayed during this time because of lower than thought of inventory.

Please note that our shipping policy may change from time to time – should our shipping policy change, so check in regularly before ordering.
